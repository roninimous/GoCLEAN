Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02 April 2024

At GoClean, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients' information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about our services or when you engage in transactions with our company. The personal information we collect may include:

  • Contact details (such as name, email address, and phone number)
  • Address and location information
  • Payment and billing information (we do not store credit card numbers)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to provide, operate, and maintain our services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Communicate with you about services or support
  • Enhance and improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information. These measures include data encryption and secure servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, and other factors. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to be informed about how we are protecting your information.
